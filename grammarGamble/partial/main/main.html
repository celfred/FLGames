Grammar Gamble Configuration

Game rules

You start with 100 dollars. For each sentence, decide if it's Right or Wrong. If 'Wrong', write a correction for the wrong part.

Place your bet : 5, 10, 15... and wait for the answer... Then, count your points !

If your answer AND your correction are correct : add your bet!

If your answer is correct, but NOT your correction : add 5.

If your answer is wrong : substract your bet :(

Start the game!
