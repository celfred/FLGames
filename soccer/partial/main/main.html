Game rules
  • 1 player in each team is randomly chosen.
  • A question is displayed.
  • The fastest player to give a correct answer passes the ball.
  • Don't raise your hand ! Repeat if the teacher asks you so ! 'Steal' the answer from the opponent by speaking faster ! Change your mind...
  • Wrong answer ? The ball stays where it is :(
  • Correct answer ? A click on the player's name passes the ball to the teammates. Be lucky : 1, 2, 3 passes? Short or long?
  • Will you be in the winning team?
  • If a player is too loud during the game : Yellow and Red cards are available by clicking on the player's name in the lists.
